Make a sleep timer app for android.

when opening app it shows a timer, the timer is hh:mm:ss

the app remembers the last timer data the user put and it will be the default untill changed.

timer continues even when app minimized

when the timer reaches 00:00:00 it will slowly lower the media volume to 0 and stop all media playeing and turn off the screen or lock the phone if possible.

theres a reset button, when clicked it resets the timer to the default data.


the notification shows even when the app is minimized/in the background

the notification shows even if the phone is locked 

the app has a Quick Settings Tile button that starts the app if its closed and starts the defualt timer
